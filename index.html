Who we Are

Nzuko ndi Igbo Brescia is a socio-cultural association founded in 1999 by the Igbo speaking Nigerians residing in Brescia, Italy. In a distant country, we believed it wise for brothers and sisters to come together in an effort to uplift the Igbo culture and interact as a family in a home far away from home. The driving force for the decision, however, was for the collective sakes of our children born in a foreign land, as a means to teach them our culture, bearing in mind the wise words of Marcus Garvey:

“A people without the knowledge of their past history, origin and culture is like a tree without roots.

Our Goals

  • We seek to instill the core values and traditions of the Igbo culture in our foreign society, as well as our children.

  • We want to promote peace, loyalty and a sense of unity amongst our members, as we believe every Igbos should share the same sentiments and ideals.

  • We wish to look out for the general welfare of our members and relations both home and abroad, as we believe that there is no better admonition other than sticking to the phrase, "We rise by lifting others."
